This publication investigates the application of reinforcement learning in robotic control systems, particularly in the context of wearable robotics and rehabilitation. The work includes:

- Novel reinforcement learning algorithms for human-in-the-loop systems
- Real-time adaptation strategies
- Experimental validation in rehabilitation settings
- Performance analysis and comparison with traditional control methods
